What it does

Battery physics, not black-box curves

Every model is grounded in peer-validated electrochemistry — OCV lookup tables, Arrhenius degradation rates, and coupled thermal dynamics.

⚡
Equivalent Circuit Model (ECM)
R₀ internal resistance + R₁–C₁ Thevenin network. OCV–SOC lookup with temperature correction. Captures transient voltage response during pulse loads and regen braking.
🌡️
Coupled Thermal Model
Lumped capacitance thermal solver. Joule heating I²R₀ + exothermic reaction heat. Convective cooling boundary conditions. Temperature feeds back into ECM parameters.
📊
SOC & SOH Estimation
Coulomb counting with OCV correction for SOC. Capacity fade and internal resistance rise modeled over cycle number. Extended Kalman filter-ready architecture.
🔋
CC-CV Charge Simulation
Full constant-current / constant-voltage charge protocol. Visualize voltage, current, and SOC vs. time. Compare charge time across C-rates and temperature setpoints.
📉
Degradation & Aging
SEI layer growth (Arrhenius kinetics), lithium plating risk at low temperatures, calendar aging. Capacity fade and resistance rise vs. cycle count — plot lifetime trajectories.
🏗️
Pack Configuration
Define series–parallel cell arrangement. Compute pack-level voltage, capacity, energy, and internal resistance. Cell-to-cell variation and imbalance analysis.
